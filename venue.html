Directions

TRAVELLING FROM CAPE TOWN (N1)

  • Travel along the N1 direction Paarl
  • Take exit 47, Klapmuts/Stellenbosch
  • Turn left onto the R44 towards Wellington
  • Drive for25km and cross over the 4way stop
  • Continue along the R44 for another 3km’s then turn left at the traffic light. (Distillery road)
  • Pass Wellington Industrial and turn right onto Oakdene road.
  • At the T-junction turn left and immediately right. Notice the Langkloof Roses sign.
  • The road becomes a dirt road. Follow the dirt road for approx.3.5km until you reach a turn off on your right onto the Slangrivier Road.
  • You’ll find the entrance to Langkloof Roses on your left.
